About this project

This project is based on a Europe wide cross border investigation. Over the last few months we have finding out how one of the most obscure processes taking place in the internet works. We have delivered some answers which have prompted new questions. During the following weeks, we will publish several articles analysing the results of our investigation across different European media.

As the Right to be Forgotten is an open issue, which will be gaining momentum in the future as more and more people will be concerned about the pros and cons to be online, we will be to keeping an eye on its evolution.

If you have any story, any complaint or any data regarding the right to be forgotten to share with us, we are happy to hear from you. You can write to us via this form or to the following email thertbfproject@hushmail.com .

All the information supplied will be anonymous.
