An investigation into the effects of the Right to be Forgotten on European news media.
According to the European Court of Justice, internet search engines like Google need to remove links from searches for peoples' names on request, for example if they're no longer relevant.
Google has to notify the respective media when such links to their sites are removed from certain searches.We collected some of this information and these are our results.
